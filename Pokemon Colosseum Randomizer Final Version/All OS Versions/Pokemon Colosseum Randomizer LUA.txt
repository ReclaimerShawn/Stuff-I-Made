AboutText=[[
All this program does is use the OS Clock and puts that through a formula to make a seed, then based on that generates values within the range of applicable things in the game.
In order to use this program, open up Pokemon Colosseum in Dolphin emulator and wait until you see "Press Any Button To Continue"
Afterwards, press hook game one time (or it might not work properly)
If you press 1 and 2 when a battle is supposed to end, you can continue the battle! The Difficulty Scaler has several modes. Pitiful mode spawns Level 1 Pokemon that are a 1 hit knock-out. Normal Mode spawns Pokemon within a Level 50 stat range. Intermediate Mode generates Pokemon within a Level 75 stat range. Hard Mode generates Pokemon at a Level 100 range.
Impossible mode makes Pokemon have an HP stat of 32767 and all other stats are 65535 (Making them impossible to kill save for one or two methods...)
Finally, Regular Mode sets levels and stats of Pokemon to what they'd normally be, but the program still randomizes their attacks and species.
Do not use the difficulty scaler in the middle of a battle; scale it after the battle ends if you don't like the mode you're on at the time. If you choose to not check randomize shadow Pokemon, shadow Pokemon species and stats will still be modified, but shadow Pokemon will always appear in their typical places. Instead of being caught at a Level based on what a Shadow Pokemon was supposed to be at (due to the shadow flag), this game allows you to catch pokemon at any level.
This means that if you catch a Level 100 shadow pokemon, it'll be Level 100 instead of what it was supposed to be. Because of this, I made the tool write EXP to the highest possible stat it could be at that level. This can cause pokemon to level up
more than intended, but pokemon still level up within the general range of where they were caught. They always gain a bit of EXP over what they were supposed to in order to prevent an infinite soft-lock. This trainer also allows the randomization
of Hyper Mode in enemy Shadow Pokemon, which increases the chances of the opponent getting a critical hit dramatically. They also have zero chance of disobeying their trainer and will always use Shadow Rush if they enter Hyper Mode.
The drop-down box that says "Shadow Pokemon" not only effects the chances of a pokemon becoming shadow, but it also effects the chances of said shadow going into Hyper Mode. For instance, if there's a 50% chance of finding a
Shadow Pokemon, there is also a 50% chance of that Shadow Pokemon entering into an obedient Hyper Mode. You can also choose not to check Shadow Pokemon Randomization, and if you do so while enabling Hyper Mode Randomization,
pokemon that are supposed to be encountered Shadow will also appear with a chance of being Hyper Mode according to the "Shadow Pokemon" drop-down box.
Finally, do not click on any of the buttons before the game is hooked. You will get a stack overflow and will have to taskill the exe. Then, you'd have to reopen both the program and a new process of Dolphin (Closing the old one out).
